Decorate the World

16 October 2016

Recently I had the opportunity to speak at NEJS CONF about javascript decorators. It was an awesome experience. JavaScript decorators give you an easy way to markup your code with a declarative. They are now used by most of the new frontend frameworks, but you can also write your own. You can learn how by watching my presentation or by looking at my slides and examples.
